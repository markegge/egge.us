  A free peer-benchmarking platform for US public transit agencies. Using National Transit Database data, agencies search and filter 2,300+ peers by size, cost, ridership, or service characteristics, then compare ridership, operating expense, farebox recovery, and cost per trip in interactive time-series and bar charts with CSV export. Covers 2019–2024 across all modes.
